# Code of Conduct

TRACE-MAS is intended to be a respectful research and engineering space.

Participants are expected to:

- communicate with care and assume good faith;
- keep technical criticism specific and actionable;
- avoid harassment, personal attacks, or discriminatory language;
- respect privacy and do not share private credentials, datasets, or messages.

Project maintainers may remove comments, issues, or contributions that violate
these expectations.
